HOW TO BUY

create a wallet

First, you'll need a crypto wallet. Grab MetaMask for free from your app store or as a Chrome extension at metamask.io. It's your gateway to the crypto world!

get some eth

Next, you'll need some Ethereum (ETH). You can easily buy ETH directly within MetaMask, transfer it from another wallet, or purchase it from a cryptocurrency exchange and then send it to your MetaMask wallet.

go to uniswap

Now, head over to Uniswap at app.uniswap.org. Once there, connect your MetaMask wallet. You'll then need to paste the official $DAIFU contract address into the token selection field, select $DAIFU when it appears, and confirm the connection.

switch eth for $DAIFU

Finally, you can swap your ETH for $DAIFU. There are zero taxes on $DAIFU transactions, offering pure profit potential. Remember to adjust your slippage settings as needed, and get ready to ride the bullish wave!
